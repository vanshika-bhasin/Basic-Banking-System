Safety BANK

Safety Bank allows you to view users, transfer money to other account and view transaction history all at one place! To view a user's account, just click the Users button below or in the navigation bar. To transfer money to some other account, you just need to select the account to credit and type in the amount to be transferred. To view transaction history just click view transaction history button and you will see all the transactions that have taken place. All the functionalities are available at the click of a button!
